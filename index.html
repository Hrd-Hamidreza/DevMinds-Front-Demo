About Us

The Mind Developers Academy was established in the fall of 1400 with the aim of identifying and training talented individuals in the field of programming and website and software development. The first activity of our academy was to identify and train talented individuals in programming at universities and child and adolescent talent agencies. In the spring of 1401, the members of the academy formed a specialized team in the field of IT. This team began its activity with the aim of designing and developing diverse and attractive software for users and subsequently held various training courses to train students and individuals interested in programming to enter the job market.
